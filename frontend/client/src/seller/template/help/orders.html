A sales order is a record of a transaction that occurs when you sell products or services to a buyer. Unpaid sales orders represent buyers' non-empty shopping carts. Unpaid sales orders aged more than 15 days are automatically deleted.
Here are few important guidelines to help you with your sales orders:
- always make sure that you have received an accurate payment for a sales order marked as "Payment Completed" (you are solely responsible for any payment discrepancies that may occur in relation to your sales orders);
- never ship products or deliver services for sales orders that were not paid (you are solely responsible for acting on your sales orders accordingly);
- use sales order's messages box to communicate to a customer in the context of the order (you are solely responsible for the consequences of communicating with your customers).
Your sales orders will appear ordered the way they were updated.
Click on a sales order to access its details.
