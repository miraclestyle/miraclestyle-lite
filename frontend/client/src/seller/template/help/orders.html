A sales order is a record of a transaction that occurs when you sell products or services to a buyer. A sales order is automatically generated when a buyer initiates a payment on shopping cart checkout. Buyers' non-empty shopping carts aged more than 15 days are automatically emptied.
Here are few important guidelines to help you with your sales orders:
- always make sure that you have received an accurate payment for an order marked as "Payment Completed" (you are solely responsible for any payment discrepancies that may occur in relation to your orders);
- never ship products or deliver services for orders that were not paid (you are solely responsible for acting on your sales orders accordingly);
- use order's messages box to communicate to a customer in the context of the order (you are solely responsible for the consequences of communicating with your customers).
Your buyers' non-empty shopping carts and sales orders will appear ordered the way they were updated.
Click on a shopping cart / sales order to access its details.
