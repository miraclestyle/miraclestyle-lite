Seller contents are visible to the general public on your seller profile. Use contents to communicate valuable information pertaining your business to customers. It is a good practice to include contents that explain at least your business conduct, rules, and policies such as the following:
- Shipping policy;
- Return policy;
- Payment policy;
- Contacts;
- About your business.
Seller contents will appear ordered the way you have manually ordered them.
Click the "Add" action to add new content.
Click on content to edit it.
Use to reorder content by dragging it within its group.
Use to remove content by dragging it outside the left edge of the screen.
