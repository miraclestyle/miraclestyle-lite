Saved addresses are a convenience that improves your shopping cart experience. Use saved address to populate shopping cart addressing fields, instead of doing so manually. Saved addresses will appear ordered the way you have manually ordered them.
Click the "Add" action to add a new address.
Click on an address to edit it.
Use reorder to reorder an address by dragging it within its group.
Use reorder to remove an address by dragging it outside the left edge of the screen.
