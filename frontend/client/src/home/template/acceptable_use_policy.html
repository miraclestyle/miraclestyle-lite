Miraclestyle is a shopping place. To make sure people feel safe and comfortable here, we require everyone to agree to and follow these rules. Note that, however, these rules are rarely black and white-for each piece of content we have to consider all of its aspects, as well as the surrounding context. If there's a question about whether something's appropriate for our community, we'll err on the side of taking it down. Stuff you can't post/sell You aren't allowed to post/sell anything that:
  • Is sexually explicit or pornographic, exploits or presents minors in a sexual way, or promotes adult sexual services;
  • Creates a genuine risk of physical injury or property damage, credibly threatens people or public safety, or organizes, encourages or celebrates harm;
  • Promotes self-harm, eating disorders or hard drug abuse;
  • Attacks, bullies or harasses nonpublic people;
  • Includes hate speech;
  • Is gratuitously violent or gory;
  • Infringes anyone's intellectual property, privacy or other rights;
  • Is fraudulent or deceptive;
  • Is someone else's personal information or requests a minor's personal information;
  • Contains any information or content that's illegal.
Things you can't do You also aren't allowed to:
  • Use Miraclestyle's name, logo, trademark or branding without our consent (our brand guidelines tell you how to use these things);
  • Access, tamper with or use non-public areas of Miraclestyle, our systems or our technical providers' systems;
  • Break or circumvent our security measures or otherwise test the vulnerability of our systems or networks;
  • Use any method to access, search, scrape, download or change Miraclestyle or anything on it (software and tools provided by Miraclestyle or popular browsers are okay);
  • Post large amounts of unwanted or repetitive stuff, post unsolicited content, etc., or try to artificially boost views or other metrics;
  • Use affiliate or redirect links that create a confusing experience for users;
  • Use meta tags, hidden text or metadata with our trademark, logo, URL or product name without our written consent;
  • Use Miraclestyle user information to forge any TCP/IP packet header or any part of the header information in any email or newsgroup posting, or in any way use Miraclestyle to send altered source-identifying information;
  • Try to reverse engineer any of Miraclestyle's software;
  • Try to interfere with any Miraclestyle user, host or network, for example by sending a virus, overloading, spamming or mail-bombing;
  • Collect or store personally identifiable information from Miraclestyle or its users without their permission;
  • Impersonate or misrepresent your affiliation with any person or entity, including Miraclestyle;
  • Do anything that violates applicable law or regulations;
  • Let anyone access your account or do anything that might put your account at risk;
  • Sell your account or otherwise transfer it for compensation;
  • Create or show ads that look like or could be confused with Miraclestyle content (for example, embedding Miraclestyle actions, follow or unfollow in your ads);
  • Compensate or incentivize users to take actions on Miraclestyle such as following;
  • Encourage or help anyone do any of the things on this list.
If you come across a content that seems to break these rules, you can report it to us. We may remove anything for any reason, including if we think it goes against any of these rules or our Terms of Service.
