Use product stock configurations to control which product variant is available for purchase. When customer customizes the product by selecting desired variant options on the product view form, product availability will be altered according to the first product stock configuration that matches the customized product.
Product stock configurations will appear ordered the way you have manually ordered them.
Click the "Add" button to add a new stock configuration.
Click on a stock configuration to edit it.
Use to reorder a stock configuration by dragging it within its group.
Use to remove a stock configuration by dragging it outside the left edge of the screen.
