Use product variants to define customizable product features. Product variants can be related to any product feature such as size, color, warranty period, etc. Product variants will enable customers to customize product by selecting desired variant options on the product view form during purchase.
Product variants will appear ordered the way you have manually ordered them.
Click the "Add" action to add a new variant.
Click on a variant to edit it.
Use reorder to reorder a variant by dragging it within its group.
Use reorder to remove a variant by dragging it outside the left edge of the screen.
