Use product variants to define customizable product features. Product variants can be related to any product feature such as size, color, warranty period, etc. Product variants will enable customers to customize product by selecting desired variant options on product view form during purchase.
Product variants will appear in the order you have manually ordered them.
Click "Add" action to add new variant.
Click on a variant to edit variant.
Use drag handle to reorder a variant by dragging it within its group.
Use drag handle to remove a variant by dragging it outside the left edge of the screen.
