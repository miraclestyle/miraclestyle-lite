Use product contents to communicate additional product information to customers. Product contents may encompass product information such as user manual, warranty, specification, features, etc.
Product contents will appear ordered the way you have manually ordered them.
Click the "Add" action to add new content.
Click on content to edit it.
Use reorder to reorder content by dragging it within its group.
Use reorder to remove content by dragging it outside the left edge of the screen.
