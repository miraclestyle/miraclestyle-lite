Use product contents to communicate additional product information to your prospective customers. Product contents may encompass product information such as user manual, warranty, specification, features, etc.
Product contents will appear in the order you have manually ordered them.
Click "Add" action to add new content.
Click on a content to edit content.
Use drag handle to reorder a content by dragging it within its group.
Use drag handle to remove a content by dragging it outside the left edge of the screen.
