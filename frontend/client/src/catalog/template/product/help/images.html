Images play perhaps the most important role in terms of sales and marketing success on the Miraclestyle app. That is why you should make sure your images look visually pleasing and impeccable while conveying desired information at the same time. Product images are an essential part of the product information.
Here are few important guidelines to help you with product images preparation:
- upload at least one image of the product;
- images should be of overall high quality, preferably in Full HD resolution, for an optimal viewing experience on a variety of displays;
- upload images on which product is readily distinguishable;
- an image should contain enough product details to make a product recognizable;
- image contents should be optimized for viewing experience on small display sizes, i.e. make sure image contents are recognizable while viewed on small display sizes.
Product images will appear ordered the way you have manually ordered them.
Click to select new images for upload.
Use to reorder an image by dragging it within its group.
Use to remove an image by dragging it outside the left edge of the screen.
