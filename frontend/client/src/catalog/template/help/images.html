Images on Miraclestyle platform play perhaps the most important role in terms of sales and marketing success, and that is why you should make sure your images look visually pleasing and impeccable while conveying desired information at the same time. Catalog images represent catalog pages arranged one next to another while catalog is being viewed. Catalog images should be processed in such manner as if they were to be printed pages of a mail order catalog or magazine.
Here are few important guidelines to help you with catalog images preparation:
- first image, the one at the upper left corner of the images grid, will serve as the catalog cover and should be of 1:1 aspect ratio (this ratio is enforced by the system);
- maintain the same aspect ratio across all non-cover images (they don't have to be 1:1 ratio);
- images should be of high overall quality, preferably in Full HD resolution, in order to provide an optimal viewing experience on variety of displays;
- avoid uploading multiple images with the same contents, e.g. same product or combination of products presented from different angles;
- image should contain enough product details to make product recognizable;
- image contents should be optimized for viewing experinece on small display sizes, i.e. make sure image contents are recognizable while viewed on small display sizes.
Catalog images will appear in the order you have manually ordered them.
Click "Upload" action to select new images for upload.
Use drag handle to reorder an image by dragging it within its group.
Use drag handle to remove an image by dragging it outside the left edge of the screen.
